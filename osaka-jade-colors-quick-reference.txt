================================================================================
                            HARMONY & THEORY
================================================================================

Color Story:
- Dominated by deep forest greens and warm earth tones
- Jade/turquoise accents provide energy and focus
- Cream/beige text ensures readability
- Rose tones add warmth and personality

Suggested Usage:
- Background:      #111C18 (all backgrounds)
- Text:            #C1C497 (body text)
- Accents:         #2DD5B7 (links, focus states)
- Highlights:      #E5C736 (alerts, important)
- Borders:         #214237 (UI dividers)

RGB Values for Hyprland/Wallpaper Engines:
- Primary:         rgb(17, 28, 24)
- Accent:          rgb(45, 213, 183)
- Highlight:       rgb(229, 199, 54)
